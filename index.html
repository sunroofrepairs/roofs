Our Services

Water leakage

When the water drainage is blocked or damaged the water overflow and get inside the car and leaving watermarks on the interior surface, wih our expect technicians they can fix within few hours.

Stuck sunroof

Stuck sunroof can be caused by many mechanical failures such as damage motor, broken parts or electrical system failure, but our expect technicians can repair it to function again.

Sunroof glass replacement

Sunroofs glasses are about four times stronger than regular glass but their not immune from breaking. We have all types of glass models and whenever you buy sunroof glass from us we install it for free.

Foldable roof failures

Foldable roof can have many failures such as non alignment, lock system failure, hydraulic failures, does not open or close properly.
